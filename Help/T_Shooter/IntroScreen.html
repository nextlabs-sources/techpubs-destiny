Introducing the Policy Troubleshooter

Welcome to the Control Center Policy Troubleshooter. As you start using Policy Studio to construct and test policies, you will likely encounter cases when you define a policy that seems to be structurally correct, yet when you deploy it to your test PCs, you do not see the expected results. Your policy may not stop a user from doing some action you intended to prohibit, for example, or it may block some action that should be allowed.

This Policy Troubleshooter is available to help you out in such cases. It is designed to guide you through a series of questions that can help isolate, understand and correct the problem. Each step in the sequence describes a potential source of policy malfunctions; at each step you should check whether the described issue does or does not apply in your case. If you find one that does, correct it, redeploy and retest the policy, and your problem may be solved. If that does not solve the problem, resume the troubleshooting sequence where you left off.
