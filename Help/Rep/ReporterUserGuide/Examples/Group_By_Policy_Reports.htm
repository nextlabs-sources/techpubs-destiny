Group By Policy Reports

As we just saw, four policies are reflected in the details report above. This means when we re-run the report with Show Group By Policy selected, we get a bar chart with four bars, one per policy. For each, the height is the same, indi­cating one enforcement instance apiece.

 

Group By Policy Report

This format is useful for identifying policies that are being triggered with unex­pected frequency, which may be an indication that they are improperly designed and cover users, resources or actions that they should not. It can also be an indi­cation of concentrated efforts at unauthorized data access. To look more closely into the latter possibility, it is often helpful to switch to Group by User format, to focus on who is performing the activity.
