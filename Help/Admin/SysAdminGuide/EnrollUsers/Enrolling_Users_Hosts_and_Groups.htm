Enrolling Users, Hosts, and Groups

Among the most important activities for Control Center administrators is enroll­ing—that is, importing—various entities into the system’s database. This must be performed after installation and is a crucial step in setting up your Control Cen­ter system. However, it is also an important part of ongoing administrative responsibilities, as you may need to enroll entities such as location sites, appli­cations or SharePoint groups at any time after your initial installation.

This section covers details on all aspects of enrolling users, groups, and host groups from your LDAP directory. This section also contains conceptual informa­tion about enrollment in general. The conceptual sections (designated with an “About” below), provide important information about what can be enrolled (dif­ferent types of enrollments), how enrollment works, and information about nec­essary input files and command line options. The procedural sections (designated with an “How to” below), provide step-by-step instructions specific to main enrollment tasks for Users, Hosts, and Groups only.
